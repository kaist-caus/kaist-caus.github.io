About CAUS

Fostering interdisciplinary collaboration to build the next generation of smart cities.

This research project aims to develop core technologies for advancing future urban artificial intelligence through an industry-academia collaboration between KAIST and GS E&C. Through this, we seek to enhance smart city development and operation technologies and prepare for the transition to future cognitive cities. We share the common goals of developing human-centric, cognition-based urban technologies and increasing global visibility.

Considering the organic and complex nature of cities, we have formed a convergent research team at KAIST encompassing civil & environmental engineering, computer science, artificial intelligence, and industrial engineering. We also collaborate with world-class research institutions in the urban IT field, such as Stanford, MIT, and NYU.
